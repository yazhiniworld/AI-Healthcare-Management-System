# AI-Enabled Smart Healthcare Management and Appointment System

## 1. Problem Statement

Many hospitals still rely on manual appointment booking and patient management systems. Patients often face difficulties in scheduling appointments, finding suitable doctors, and maintaining their medical records. Manual processes can lead to appointment conflicts, long waiting times, and inefficient record management.

To address these challenges, a web-based healthcare management system is proposed. The system enables patients to register, view doctors, book appointments, and track appointment history. It also helps hospitals manage doctors, patients, and appointments efficiently. The platform is designed to support future AI-based healthcare features such as symptom analysis and doctor recommendation.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

1. To develop a web-based healthcare management system.
2. To provide secure patient registration and login functionality.
3. To allow patients to view available doctors and book appointments online.
4. To maintain doctor and patient information efficiently.
5. To manage appointment scheduling and appointment history.
6. To provide an admin dashboard for managing healthcare operations.
7. To design a scalable system that can support future AI healthcare services.

---

## 3. Users of the System

### Patient

* Register account
* Login to the system
* View doctors
* Book appointments
* Cancel appointments
* View appointment history

### Doctor

* Login to the system
* View assigned appointments
* Update appointment status
* Manage availability

### Admin

* Manage patients
* Manage doctors
* Monitor appointments
* Generate reports
* Maintain system records
